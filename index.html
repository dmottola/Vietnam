A bike trip through Vietnam is an experience. From a modern, bustling capital, to the rural farmlands and rice paddies, you will never find better sights and twistier roads than Northern Vietnam. A true challenge for any level of rider, this journey is a must for all who love seeing the world on two wheels. Start the journey >

Northern Vietnam

Northern Vietnam is characterized by huge limestone karsts, rural farmland, twisting switchbacks up and down mountains, and often unfinished roads. In short, perfect for a good rough ride.
